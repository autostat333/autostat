About Project

This project has been finished in a 6 month. Entirely, I have spent about 360 hours, and it is 1-2 months of full-time engagement (process, design, front-end, backend).

Product is designated to help for public understand the avarage prices on cars when someone will be choosing car for purchasing or sellings his own. The history is very simple and relies on human nature, which are in many cases reduced to simply

buy some respectable things for cheaper price.

Traversing the advertisements of cars I figured out that it would be better to have kind of aggregated totals about particular car or model to make possible compare with another one of the same class....which one among them will be cheaper....(e.g. Infinity FX 2010 VS Mersedes ML 2010) Here was the idea which I have been starting to pursuit. Now it is finished.

Within this project you can:

figure out how much your car worth, and what you can buy having some amount of money.

Surely, additionally you can track for trends, and your choice (selected cars to tabs) is saving locally.

Across entire project - I was trying to use different kind of toggles, datepickers etc. To say shortly - bring in project enought interactivnesses. And a lot of features I am planning to do, which have not implemented yet because of leak of time. But it is all - the future. Hope I will imporove this product

Product has been developed and tested in Chrome browser. It may contain some bugs (obviously), I am working to gather all and fix.
